Bus schedule and location for AMW Thursday sessions in Colonia del Sacramento

To attend Thursday sessions in Colonia del Sacramento (including lunch and visit), AMW will provide bus transportation.
Buses will leave from the parking lot behind the conference site (see map). Boarding will start at 8:45am, please arrive at the parking lot on time. The last bus will leave by 9am.
The return buses will come back to the same parking lot location, arriving in Montevideo by 9:30 p.m.
